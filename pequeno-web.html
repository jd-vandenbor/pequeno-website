Features

GPS Tracking

24/7 tracking from any location around the world directly from the app. Using the Pequeno wireless service, you are able to set safe zones and receive mobile alerts upon safe zone exit or entry.

Fall Detection

The Pequeno Guardian is able to send outgoing alerts upon sudden rapid movements that may signify a fall in the wearer. The three emergency contacts that you set will receive the alerts and can then follow up with the wearer to ensure the safety of your loved one.

4G Wireless Service

With 4G broadband cellular network technology, you can ensure that you and your loved ones are always sending and receiving quick and up-to-date alerts when it matters most.
